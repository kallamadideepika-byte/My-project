About the Temple

The Templo de San Francisco, built in the 16th century, is one of Lima's most important colonial monuments. Famous for its stunning Baroque architecture and the famous catacombs beneath, it attracts thousands of visitors annually.

The church features intricate wood carvings, beautiful tile work, and serves as a perfect example of colonial Peruvian architecture. The catacombs contain thousands of bones arranged in artistic patterns.
